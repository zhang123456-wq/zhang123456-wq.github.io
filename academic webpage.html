Biography
        I am a doctoral candidate majoring in Transportation Planning and Management at the School of Transportation Engineering, Chang'an University. My supervisor is Professor Yan Li. I obtained a master's degree from Xi'an University of Architecture and Technology under the supervision of Professor Congying Li, and a bachelor's degree from the School of Civil Engineering at Henan University of Technology.
        My current research direction is the resilience of transportation environment and shared bicycle travel scheduling, including experimental methods, optimization modeling, solving algorithms, and other aspects. I am committed to improving the quality of travelers' travel through the optimization of transportation microenvironment.
